# A stupid cute cat

Somewhere in Brussels, lives a stupid cute cat.
He lives in a small apartment, but their humans made everthing they could to made it cat friendly: a scratching post that reaches the ceiling, a window bed where observe runners and people on bikes from, another windw bed but more comfortable, toys...and he choses leftover carboard boxes from amazon.
